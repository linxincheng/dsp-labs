    Who Pried the Cake of Data Storage

    Sean fu
    May 28 · 11 min read

    Author:Sean Fu

    “For data, the full cycle of property rights confirmation and friction cost reduction can achieve the optimization of data transaction value.”

    HIDDEN DANGERS OF DATA GROWTH

    Data production creates value. With the rapid development and in-depth application of data technologies such as mobile internet, social media, Internet of Things, cloud computing, artificial intelligence, etc., massive, diverse and real-time data has emerged. Based on the analysis of these data, people have taken a series of decisions and actions, which has achieved remarkable results in the consumer market, epidemic disease prediction, traffic management and other fields. These behaviors based on data value analysis gradually change the way we live, study, and work, and expand and penetrate rapidly in all aspects of society and industries. Data as a valuable commodity is playing an increasingly important role in human society. These valuable data are stored in large amounts in the cloud of major manufacturers, which constitute a huge cloud storage market.

    According to data from multiple institutions, the global cloud storage market maintained a high growth rate of more than 20% from 2016 to 2019. The global cloud storage market is expected to reach more than US $ 270 billion in 2022. Although the scale and users of the cloud storage market are growing rapidly, as the main business model of cloud storage-centralized storage is exposing more and more defects.

    1. Data cannot be confirmed: Service providers lack the means and motivation for copyright protection of user data.
    2. Lack of personal privacy protection: Industry data is now very important, competitors often find ways to obtain data, and the leakage or sale of user data often allows data storage service providers to obtain huge benefits. And because the file is stored in a single or distributed network or server in the form of whole or slice, it is easier to be attacked, and data leakage accidents are also increasing year by year. There are hacker attacks and malicious actions caused by the operators themselves.
    3. The risk of service providers make off with money: the current cloud storage market is also very competitive. Due to the increase in the number of users, in order to ensure a good user experience, the cost of service providers has increased, and there is no good profitable means for the time being. News of merchants running or stopping services is not uncommon, but users are unable to have any restraint and claim on the behavior of service providers. This has resulted in users tending to store data at larger, more credit-worthy service providers, and the degree of data centralization is increasing.
    4. Difficulties in realizing data: Due to the centralized storage space and bandwidth are purchased by service providers for their own business, the saved data cannot use these infrastructures to participate in market activities, there is no copyright mechanism for the stored data, and the value of the data is valued. The path is uncertain, the liquidity is reduced, and the value of limited exchange data is not high.

    SEPARATION OF DATA PERSISTENCE AND FLOW

    It is precisely because of the above problems that data island issues arise. In the existing centralized storage, the design is centered on network disks / software / applications. Each service provider, each enterprise department, and each user logical space are independently stored, maintained, and isolated from each other. Due to the lack of power confirmation mechanism, there is no unified standard for network protocols, different data maintenance methods among different service providers, the consistency of the data exchange process is also not unified, and the relationship between network resources is more difficult The contradiction of coordination, which leads to the normal state of data sharing, is often the data persistence layer-> user layer-> data persistence layer, resulting in high transaction costs and time costs. In order to solve the data island, people began to use decentralized protocols and blockchain technology to confirm the data and encourage shared data.

    There are about three decentralized solutions:

    1. Partially centralized storage solution. This type of solution is generally the product of the combination of early distributed storage and blockchain. Users lease their own space to operators. Operators carry out user distribution through centralized operation and blockchain token mechanism, making users get an experience similar to centralized storage, while storage costs are greatly reduced. But because it is a centralized operation, in addition to greatly reducing storage costs, the above four problems cannot be solved.
    2. Decentralized storage protocol: Decentralized storage is a storage model that uses distributed storage technology to store files or shards on the space provided by different ecological participants. It advocates privacy protection, low cost, data redundancy backup, high-speed sharing and other value propositions, open source programs, and community governance models, which are conducive to avoiding single points of failure and data value transfer. In response to the above centralized storage problem, decentralized storage is based on blockchain technology. Through off-chain storage and API interfaces, the TPS of the storage network is greatly improved on the basis of the existing public chain and alliance chain; and, through Encourage and punish ecological participants to comply with certain operating rules and require no or few centralized governance institutions. Since copyright is confirmed by the blockchain, the value of data can flow freely on the chain. And has a certain sharing ability, in the process of multiple nodes sharing data with each other, to achieve shared distribution of shards outside the storage node, and continuously expand the number of nodes in the file sharing network. Therefore, the decentralized storage system makes up for the shortcomings of the slow transmission speed of the centralized storage system, while overcoming single points of failure and ensuring data security. However, due to the existence of data shards in the form of DHT tables in the storage protocol, data is searched through a centralized retrieval service. At the same time, due to the data on-chain and the existence of various transaction fees, friction costs are higher, and the sharing distribution process The nodes in the database also have no good incentives, and are more suitable for storing “cold” data that is not particularly frequent.
    3. Decentralized sharing protocol: This type of protocol is represented by the BT protocol, the storage network adopts the P2P mode, and the retrieval protocol adopts the DHT + centralized traker method, which is convenient for users to retrieve data, and there is almost no transaction fee, so a large number of users participate. However, the existing shared protocol network has no definite persistence agreement for data storage and no clear value transfer channel for data exchange. It is purely through online time and the mode of measuring shared contributions to obtain incentives, although the resources in the sharing process are solved The prisoner’s dilemma, but the workload lacks consensus across the network, and there is no more reasonable economic model design. At the same time, there is also a lack of control over data rights, so copyright data cannot be shared and distributed under the premise of compliance. Due to the characteristics of the sharing economy, the hot data will be distributed in large quantities, and the cold data will not be motivated to maintain or even “disappear” in the network, so it is more suitable for “hot” data storage.

    It can be seen that the existing storage / sharing protocols have more or less solved many problems of centralized storage, but they still cannot beat the centralized storage model in terms of availability and cost-effectiveness. In addition to the uncoordinated goals of the community project parties, the main problem is also the lack of design concepts for data persistence and circulation. These protocols solve the trust and incentive problems at the storage and sharing function layer, but data persistence and flow is a closed-loop logic. The lack of consideration of a link on the entire link will affect the usability of the entire platform. DSP Labs discovered this problem from the beginning, so we tried to look at the whole problem from another angle.

    THE LIFE CYCLE OF DATA AND COASE THEOREM

    First, the data is valuable. The value of data is that for different consumers, through continuous analysis and continuous learning of the data, it can guide the next strategy and action. From this perspective, data is an important resource. As a resource, data can also be circulated in economic activities to realize its maximum value in the process of continuous value exchange. Through Coase’s law, DSP Labs gets some inspiration from the data life cycle.

    “Coase Theorem: As long as property rights are clear and transaction costs are zero or very small, no matter who gives property rights at the beginning, the final result of market equilibrium is efficient, achieving Pareto optimal resource allocation . ”

    The property right we need to determine here is the ownership of the data, and the transaction cost is the frictional cost of state transfer. Coase’s law tells us that for data, the full cycle of property rights confirmation and friction cost reduction can only optimize the value of data transactions. According to the analysis of the above three decentralized protocols, the partial centralization scheme does not have a good property rights confirmation scheme for data generation and persistence. Most of them are still based on the data they operate and the credit of the service provider. Without the premise of right confirmation, there is a problem of unclear value confirmation right in the exchange, which causes great problems in the later data confirmation, which is not conducive to data transfer. Data flow gets value. The decentralized storage agreement has done a good job in defining property rights, which defines the distribution of equity. In some projects, property rights can be determined for the first three stages, and benefits can be distributed. In the fourth stage, most of the projects are completed within consumers, and the value is not exported to the entire chain. Although the power confirmation is done well, the decentralized storage protocol still has the problem of excessive friction costs, that is, the transaction costs on the chain and the retrieval costs are too high. Sharing agreements have a long way to go in both respects.

    It can be seen that the unclear definition of data life cycle property rights and the high cost of friction restrict the closed-loop logic of the value of the entire data.

    PROBABLY BETTER STRATEGY

    DSP Labs tried to redesign the protocol from the perspective of the entire data life cycle.

    First, we need to clarify the data property rights at each stage.

    Using the blockchain, we can confirm the ownership of data, including copyright. In the blockchain, which cannot be tampered with, a unified distributed ledger across the entire network, a series of information such as the time, location, attributes, owner, etc. of data creation will be recorded in detail, and the data distribution authority can be saved to make the data clear , permissions can be controlled. This is the basis for realizing the value of data transfer. Therefore, in the persistence phase, a scalable, distributed storage network based on blocks is indispensable. It not only completes the storage function, but also is a participant in the subsequent data transfer.

    Service Warranty

    The data property rights at the data persistence and circulation stage also need to be confirmed. During the persistence period, the user purchases the service of the service node through storage fees. The service node needs to make a commitment to the user’s data integrity. This commitment indicates the property relationship of the data during the persistence period on the blockchain. In the process of transfer, it is also necessary to define the property rights of the transferred data, that is, in the data sharing process, a payment commitment that can be verified by the consensus of the entire network, and the one who receives the data pays the data fee to the other party, which represents a kind of data rights Transfer.

    In the sharing process, due to the payment commitment, the nodes involved in the link can be stimulated, so the enthusiasm of other nodes can be greatly improved and actively participate in the entire sharing process. And because the payment commitment of the data is responsible for the consensus settlement by the blockchain, the unnecessary repeated transmission will not bring additional benefits to the nodes and avoid the waste of resources.

    Agency Commitment

    In addition, we have introduced a proxy network protocol. The proxy network provides a data transfer method for a large number of devices that cannot provide a stable network address under the second-tier network. Firstly, the access threshold (cost) is reduced, and a large number of edge devices are introduced. Secondly, fixed service nodes are added to the data flow, which improves the availability of the system. Similarly, according to the requirements of the service commitment, the agent network needs the data agent commitment to get the data transfer. In fact, this is also a way to reduce friction costs (increasing the success rate of data exchange).

    Reduce friction costs

    In the process of data persistence and circulation, friction costs come from these aspects: transaction fees on the chain, third-party data retrieval fees, and data rollback caused by possible forks in the blockchain ledger.

    Due to the existence of transaction fees on the chain, the logic on the chain cannot be called frequently, because it will cause excessive friction costs. The off-chain payment, the layer2 high-speed payment protocol for on-chain settlement, can efficiently complete the off-chain block sharing payment logic, while reducing the cost of on-chain settlement. In addition, the block mapping information of the file is managed with a blockchain contract, and a distributed contract retrieval service is provided on the node that implements the layer2 payment protocol. Each data receiver, while receiving the data, can disclose the data segmentation information it owns on the payment node, other nodes can quickly obtain the segmentation information, and obtain the data segmentation by payment, and Further broadcasting fragment information, this process greatly improves the efficiency of sharing. At the same time, the payment network and the retrieval service have been merged. The user can perform the retrieval service at no cost, and the payment network has no incentive to do evil, because it will affect its payment commitments.

    Strongly consistent consensus network

    Finally, we need a fast-converging, efficient, and consistent consensus network to reduce the risk of data rollback. If there is a risk of data bifurcation or rollback, the delay of the entire platform to confirm the transaction will be very high, and the time cost is also an important friction cost. In addition, the business process will also be cancelled or timed out, which will affect the entire network Functional experience.

    CONCLUSION

    • Data production creates a lot of value, and there are a series of hidden dangers in the centralized storage business model.

    • Existing decentralized protocols have more or less fixed the shortcomings of centralized storage, using blockchain to clarify user ownership of data, which is the basis of data transfer.

    • Based on the data life cycle model, DSP Labs redesigned a distributed data protocol that may be better using Coase ’s law, including:

    Scalable and distributed storage protocol based on block

    Two-tier high-speed payment network for off-chain payment and on-chain settlement

    Address mapping logic and distributed data retrieval method based on blockchain

    Agent network protocol to lower the network access threshold

    Blockchain network with better consistency and higher efficiency

    Finally, data generation creates value, and data circulation gains value.
